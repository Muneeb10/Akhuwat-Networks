About Akhuwat Foundation

Founded on the principles of Islamic brotherhood and compassion, Akhuwat has been transforming lives through interest-free microfinance since 2001.

Our Story

Akhuwat Foundation was established with a powerful vision: to eliminate poverty through interest-free microfinance based on the Islamic principle of Qard-e-Hasna (benevolent loan). What started as a small initiative has grown into Pakistan‚Äôs largest microfinance institution.

Our unique model operates through mosques, churches, temples, and community centers ‚Äî fostering interfaith harmony while providing financial services to the underserved.

  • Largest interest-free microfinance organization in Pakistan
  • Serving over 4.5 million families nationwide
  • Zero interest policy grounded in Islamic principles
  • 99.8% loan recovery rate - proof of public trust
  • Award-winning organization with global recognition
  • Operating in all provinces with 800+ branches
